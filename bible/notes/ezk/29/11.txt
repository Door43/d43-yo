===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **No man’s foot will pass through it! No beast’s foot will pass through it! And it will not be inhabited for forty years! - **"No man or animal will live or walk through that area for forty years." (See:Metonymy)
  * **For I will make the land of Egypt a desolation in the midst of uninhabited lands, - **"No one will live in Egypt and it will be surrounded by other lands where no one lives."
  * **and its cities in the midst of wasted cities will become a desolation for forty years, - **"The cities also will be deserted for forty years."
  * **disperse them though the lands. - **"spread them among countries far away."
