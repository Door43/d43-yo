===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **uprooted**  - "pulled up from the ground"
  * **cast down**  - "thrown down"
  * **an eastern wind dried out her fruit - ** Alternate translation: "A wind blowing from the east dried out the fruit of the vine."
  * **withered**  - "dried out"
  * **So now she is planted in the wilderness, in a land of drought and thirst.**  - Though ruined, the vine lives on in a desolate place of need.
