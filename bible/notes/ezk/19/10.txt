===== Translation Notes: =====

Ezekiel returns to speaking to the princes of Israel in symbolic language.

  * **Your mother was like a vine**  - Ezekiel compares the mother of the house of Israel to a vine in order to focus on her fruitfulness. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]).
  * **Your mother**  - Ezekiel refers to the mother of the princes of Israel.
  * **abundance**  - This word means an amount more than enough.
  * **She had strong branches for ruler's scepters**  - Alternate translation: "Some of the branches were strong and became leaders" or "And those branches were good for making scepters that symbolize the power of a king." (UDB).
  * **And her heights were exalted among the branches of the thicket.**  - The vine was tall enough to be seen from a distance.
