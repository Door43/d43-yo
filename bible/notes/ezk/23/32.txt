===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will drink your sister's cup, which is deep and large **- This phrase is a metaphor. Yahweh is stating that Oholibah will drink (which means she will endure or experience) her sister's cup (the punishment that her older sister Oholab received), which is deep and large (which refers to the amount of punishment received). (See Metaphor).
  * **You **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **your **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **Sister **- This pronoun refers to Oholah, Oholibah's older sister.
  * **laughingstock **- This word indicates a foolish person.
  * **derision **- As used here, this word means a recipient of derision, that is, ridicule or criticism.
