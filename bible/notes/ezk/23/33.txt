===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your sister Samaria’s cup **-This phrase is a metaphor. Samaria represents Oholibah and the cup represents the punishment that she received. (See Metaphor).
  * **drain it empty **- This means to drink everything that is in the cup.
  * **gnaw the shards of it and rend your breasts **- alternate translation: chew it to pieces and cut your breasts
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**- See how you translated this in 5:11.
