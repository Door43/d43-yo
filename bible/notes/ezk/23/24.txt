===== Translation Notes: =====
 
  * ** they** - This pronoun refers to Oholibah's "lovers": the Babylonians, Pekod, Shoa, Koa, and the Assyrians. 
  * ** you** - This pronoun refers to Oholibah (a symbol of Jerusalem/Judah).
  * ** I** - This pronoun refers to Yahweh. 
  * ** wagons** - A synonym for "wagon" is "cart."
  * ** large shields, bucklers, and helmets ** - An alternate translation could read "large shields, small shields, and helmets." A shield is a piece of metal or wood carried for protection, to ward off arrows, rocks, etc.  A buckler is a small shield.  A helmet is a hard hat used to protect the head.
  * ** And I will set justice before them and they will judge you with their judgments!** -  "I will allow them to capture you and punish you in the way that they always punish their enemies." (UDB) An alternate translation could read, "And I will use them to perform justice and they will judge you according to their custom."
  * ** posterity** - This word means succeeding generations, i.e., children and their children on down the line.
