===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will become fuel for the fire! **- Yahweh is declaring that he will destroy the Ammonites. (See: Metaphor).
  * **fuel **- "Fuel" is anything that burns to make a fire.
  * **the land**  - This refers to the Kingdom of Ammon. Alternate translation: "your own country."
