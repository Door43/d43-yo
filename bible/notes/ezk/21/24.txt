===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore thus says the Lord Yahweh **- God is now speaking. He is telling the people of Jerusalem what is going to happen to them because of their sin.
  * **Because you bring your iniquity to my remembrance **- The people in Jerusalem are not ashamed of their sins, and everybody knows about their rebellion against the king of Babylon.
  * **your and you **- These pronouns refer to the people of Jerusalem.
  * **you will be captured by your enemy's hand** - The people of Israel will be captured by the king of Babylon.
  * **your enemy** - This refers to the king of Babylon.
