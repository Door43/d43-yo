===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came to me again and said**  - Alternate translation: "God came to Ezekiel again and said."
  * **sword**  - This means "army" (see Metonymy).
  * **The two**  - This refers to two armies from Babylon (See: Metonymy).
  * **way**  - This can also be translated "road."
  * **The two will come out from one land and a hand will clear it; it will clear the beginning of the city road** - Alternate Translation: "The two will come out from one land. Also make a sign to mark the beginning of the city road."
  * **but Judah and Jerusalem will be fortified** - Alternate translation: "and in Judah, into fortified Jerusalem"
  * **fortified**  - This means built up, made strong, and protected. Often times, this refers to a wall around a city to protect the city from attack.
