===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now you**  - God continues to speak to Ezekiel
  * **strike your hands together **- This means "clap your hands together"
  * **the sword will be doubled a third time!**- This means that the sword will strike them over and over.
  * **A sword of the slain!**  - This means a sword for killing people.
  * **It is a sword of the great one who is slain**- This symbolic sword is so great that only the greatest warrior can use it.  It is capable of the greatest destruction.
  * **My sword will strike them**- God's punishment will strike Jerusalem. (See: Metonymy)
