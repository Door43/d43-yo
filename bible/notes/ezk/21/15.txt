===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in order to melt their heart **- This is an idiom meaning to make them weak, or to make them afraid. It can also mean to produce an emotional response, but that is not what it means in this sentence.
  * **to multiply their stumbling blocks**- This is an expression meaning to make things very difficult for them
  * **their **- "Their" refers to the people of Israel that God is attacking.
  * **I have set the slaughter of the sword against their gates! **- This means that God is attacking the whole city.
  * **slaughter of the sword**  - Here, this phrase means the "killing by the sword."
  * **it is made like lightning, set free to butcher **- This means that the sword will kill, and it will do this fast.
  * **butcher**  - This word means to cut up into pieces, often in preparation for eating.
  * **Gather your selves together! Go to the right! Set yourselves up! Go to the left! Where will you order your face? **  - This means that the people will not be able to hide from God's punishment, even if they try.
  * **strike my hands together, and bring my fury to rest**  - God will quickly end the killing.
  * **Yahweh, declare it **- Here, God is making sure that it is known that He is the one who is saying these things.
