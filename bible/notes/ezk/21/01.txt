===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then the word of Yahweh came to me**  - God spoke to Ezekiel.
  * **set your face toward**  - "look toward"
  * **rant**  - to speak in an angry or emotionally charged manner.
  * **holy places**  - Alternate translation: "sanctuaries and temples"
  * **Behold!**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **will draw my sword from its sheath**  - "God is preparing to destroy Israel"
  * **sheath**  - a cover for the blade of a knife or sword.
