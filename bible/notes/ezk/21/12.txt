===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thrown onto the sword**  - "killed by the sword"
  * **slap your upper thigh**  - This indicates a physical response to intense or surprising information. Your language may have an idiom to express this idea. (See: Idiom)
  * **trial**  - This word means a proceeding, usually conducted by the government, in which opposing parties in a dispute present evidence.
  * **rod**  - This word means a tool used for discipline.
  * **declaration**  - This words means a formal announcement, either spoken or in writing.
