===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:yo:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **they were scattered** - AT: "I scattered them." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * ** and when I am sanctified in them, so the nations may see** - "I will use them to show the nations that I am holy."
  * **will make their homes** - "will live" or "will dwell"
  * **within her** - "in the land"
  * **and live securely** - "and live in safety"
  * **so they** - Possible meanings are 1) "so house of Israel" 2) "so the nations."
  * **will know that I am Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:06:06|6:7]].
  * **their God** - "the God of the house of Israel"
