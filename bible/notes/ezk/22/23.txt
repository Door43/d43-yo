===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came to me and said,**  - Alternate translations: “Yahweh's message came to me” or “Yahweh gave me this message” or “Yahweh spoke this message to me.”
  * **her**  - This pronoun refers to Jerusalem.  See usage of "her" in verse 21.
  * **day of indignation**  - An alternate translation could be "day of wrath."
  * **conspiracy**  - This word means a secret plan made by two or more people to do something harmful or illegal.
  * **like a roaring lion tearing prey, they consume life and take precious wealth!**  - God is saying that the prophets are hurting people, taking things from others and killing men.
  * **multiply**  - This word means to grow in amount, number or degree.
  * **widows**  - This word means women whose husbands have died, often leaving them in difficult situations.
