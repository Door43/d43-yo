===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So behold!**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **I/my/myself** - This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **you/your** - This pronoun refers to Jerusalem (people of Jerusalem).
  * **struck my hand**  - This means to "hit my hand".
  * **dishonest gain** - This phrase refers to making money through methods that are not honest. It could refer to cheating as well as stealing.
  * **So I will scatter you among the nations and disperse you through the lands **- Literally this means to dash into pieces and scatter to the wind. The word “scatter” means to separate and drive off in various directions. The word disperse is very similar and the two words are often used interchangeably. This is a doublet, emphasizing what God will do.  (See: Doublet)
  * **nations…lands**  - These two words refer to other places or countries.
  * **purge**  - This means to remove something unwanted.
  * **So you will be unclean within you in the eye of the nations**  - This means, "Other nations will see you as unclean."
