===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As a collection of silver and bronze, iron, lead and tin in the midst of the furnace to have fire blown against it, I will melt you.**  - The metaphor from verse 19 continues.  God explains that just like metal is processed for purification, they will be gathered for purification.
  * **I/my/mine** - This pronouns refer to Yahweh.
  * **you**  - This pronoun refers to the house (or people) of Israel.
  * **melt**  - This word means to be changed from a solid to a liquid through the application of heat.
  * **fury** - violent anger or rage, wrath
  * **so will I gather you in mine anger and in my wrath**  - The reason they have been gathered in Jerusalem for purification is because of God's anger.
  * **and I will lay you there and pour you out**  - Alternate translation: "and I will lay you there and melt you."  
  * **so you will be poured out in her midst.**  - Alternate translation: "so you will be melted in her midst."
  * **her/its**  - This pronoun refers to Jerusalem
