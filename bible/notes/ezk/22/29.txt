===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **people of the land** - the people of Israel.
    * **extortion**  - This word means abusing authority in order to obtain money from others.
    * **plunder**  - This word means to rob another of material goods by force.
    * **robbery**  - This word means to take another's belongings by force or intimidation.
    * **mistreat**  - This word means to deal with another person wrongly or roughly.
    * **oppress the sojourner without justice**  - This phrase means that the Israelites took advantage of sojourners in the legal system.
    * **sojourner**  - This word means a person who resides somewhere temporarily.
