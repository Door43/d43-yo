===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your, you - **  Refers to the Israelites (A Possessive Pronoun)
  * **shameful behavior is in your uncleanness**  - This means Israel's bad behavior or 'immoral' behavior that is unfit in God's eyes.
  * **I have cleansed you but you were not clean**-(Alternate Translation):"I cleaned you but you were not completely clean" or "I tried to cleanse you from your wicked behavior, but you did not allow me to do that."
  * **I, my**  - This is a Pronoun which refers to Yahweh.
  * **until my fury rests from you **- this means that until God calms his anger or wrath upon Israel.
