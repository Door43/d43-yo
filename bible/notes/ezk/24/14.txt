===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I-**  is a pronoun referring to Yahweh
  * **I will not relent nor will I rest from it **  - Yahweh will not become less determined or relax  from  or stop His fury.
  * **Your**  - This is a pronoun referring to the Israelites.
  * **They**  - Refers to the Babylonians.
  * **As your ways were and as your activities, they will judge you**  - The Babylonians will judge the Israelites on their ways and activities.
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration - ** See how you translated that in 5:1.
