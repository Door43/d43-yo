===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his - **  This pronoun refers to the Israelites.
  * **Serve them if you will refuse to listen to me**  - An alternate translation could be, "go ahead and serve them (idols) if you will not obey me."
  * **them - ** This pronoun refers to Israel's idols.
  * **you**  - This pronoun refers to Israel.
  * **me**  - This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
