===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** - This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **me** - This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **Is he not a speaker of parables?** - The purpose of the question was that Israel was rebuking Ezekiel's words. Alternate translation: "He is a speaker of parables. (See: Rhetorical question).
  * **I** - This pronoun refers to Ezekiel
  * **He** - This pronoun refers to Ezekiel.
