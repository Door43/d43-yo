===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Who among you fears Yahweh? Who obeys the voice of his servant?** - These are rhetorical questions meant to emphasize that they honor Yahweh and obey his servant. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Who walks in deep darkness, without light?** - This rhetorical question is comparing their enduring suffering to someone who walks in darkness with no light. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
