===== Translation Notes: =====
Isaiah continues speaking to the people of Judah.

  * **On every high hill....his wounding them.** - Yahweh will bless his people more than anyone can expect. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **in the day of the great slaughter when the towers fall** - "when Yahweh slaughters your enemies and causes their strong towers to fall"
  * **and the light of the sun will be seven times brighter, like the sunlight of seven days** â€“ "and the sun will shine as bright as seven suns." The number seven indicates perfect light from the moon and the sun.
