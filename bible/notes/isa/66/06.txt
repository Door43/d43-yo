  * **A sound of battle tumult comes from the city, a sound from the temple** - "a loud uproar like the noise of a battle comes from the city and from the temple"
  * **the sound of Yahweh paying back his enemies** - "the sound of Yahweh punishing his enemies for what they did to Israel"
