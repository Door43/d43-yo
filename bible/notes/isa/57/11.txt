===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Whom are you worried about and afraid of, that you acted so deceitfully?** - Yahweh is using this rhetorical question to tell them that they were afraid of the idols instead of fearing Yahweh and they lied to Yahweh about what they were doing. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **I was not long silent, was I?** - Yahweh asks this rhetorical question to say that they were not afraid of him because he had not punished them for a long time. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **I will proclaim your 'righteousness'** - Here Yahweh is mocking them by calling their evil deeds as 'righteous.' (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]])
