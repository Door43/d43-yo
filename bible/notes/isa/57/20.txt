===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **and its waters churn up mire and mud** - Here the wicked people are being compared to the waves in the sea. The wicked do not have inner peace and are restless just like the waves in the sea that keep churning up mud. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
