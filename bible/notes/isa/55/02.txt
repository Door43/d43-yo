===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Why do you weigh out silver for what is not bread?** - This rhetorical question is used to express Yahweh's frustration that they are using their money to buy things that they don't really need. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **And labor for what does not satisfy?** - Yahweh also uses this rhetorical question to express his frustration that they are working hard to earn money to buy things that don't satisfy their inner beings. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **listen carefully to me and eat what is good** - Here Yahweh is comparing eating what is good to getting good things. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
    * **and delight your lives in fatness** - Yahweh is comparing fatness to be truly happy in the heart. Yahweh says that if they come to him, they will be truly happy in their hearts. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
