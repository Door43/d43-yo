===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **we are the clay** - Here the people are being compared to clay, that the potter uses. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
    * **You are our potter; and we all are the work of your hand** - Yahweh is being compared to a potter, who creates things with his hand. The people of Israel were created by Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
