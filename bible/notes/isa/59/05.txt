===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **They hatch eggs of a poisonous snake...weave a spider's web ** - The people of Judah were so wicked that they made plans to harm others. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **Their webs cannot be used for garments** - The people's evil ways can not be hidden from Yahweh.
