===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He saw that there was no man, and wondered that there was no one to intervene** - “ Yahweh looked for someone to stop the evil, and he was surprised when He did not find anyone”
  * **Therefore his own arm brought salvation for him, and his righteousness sustained him** - "Yahweh used his power to save the people, and his righteousness kept him going until all was made right" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
