===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For our many transgressions are before you, and our sins testify against us;** - “In your sight our transgressions have increased greatly ,and our sins witness against us” (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **We have spoken extortion and turning aside, conceived complaining from the heart and words of lying**   – “ From our sinful hearts we have spoken words of oppression, rebellion, and falsehood."
