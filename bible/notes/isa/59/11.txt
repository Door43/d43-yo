===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We growl like bears and moan like doves** - "The people are hungry and their stomachs growl like a bear and they cry out in their suffering like the moaning sound of doves." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * ·**we wait for justice, but there is none; for salvation, but it is far from us** – “ We wait for God as a just judge to save us, but He is far from us”
