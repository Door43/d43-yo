===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore justice is far from us, nor does righteousness reach us** - “Therefore, God does not show us His favor and blessing” (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **We wait for light, but see, darkness; we look for brightness, but we walk in darkness** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **We grope for the wall like the blind, like those who cannot see. We stumble at noonday as in the twilight** - “We act as if we were blind, groping a wall for direction and stumbling at noon like it was night” (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **among the strong we are like dead men** - "like dead men in the midst of abundant life" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
