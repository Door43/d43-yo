===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Justice is driven back, and righteousness stands far off, for truth stumbles in the public square, and right cannot come** – "Justice and righteousness have been pushed far away from the courts and truth is not valued in the center of the city, and what is right is shut out”
  * **Trustworthiness has gone away, and he who turns away from evil makes himself a victim** - “Truth has no place, and he who turns away from evil makes himself open to being robbed”
