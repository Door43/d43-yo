===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **but my own arm brought victory for me** - The "arm" represents power. Yahweh has defeated their enemies with his own power. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **and my strong desire drove me on** - Here Yahweh is talking about his "strong desire" as if it were a person who can motivate him. Yahweh's passionate anger has helped him defeat their enemies. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **I trampled down the peoples in my anger and made them drunk in my wrath** - Here Yahweh compares his punishing of the nations to people who stagger from being drunk. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
