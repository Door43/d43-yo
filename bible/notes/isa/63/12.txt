===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **He who made the arm of his glory go with the right hand of Moses** - Here "arm" represents the power of Yahweh. It was through his power that Moses was able to separate the Red Sea when he raised his arm over it." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **to make himself an everlasting name?** - "with the result that he will be honored forever?"
    * **like a horse running on flat land they did not stumble** - The Israelites who crossed the Red Sea after it was parted are being compared to horses running on land without stumbling. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
