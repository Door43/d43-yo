===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **As the cattle that go down into the valley** - Here the Israelites who crossed the Red Sea are being compared to cattle that walk down into a valley to rest. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **to make yourself a name of praise** - Here "name of praise" represents honor. By leading his people, Yahweh caused himself to be greatly honored. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
