===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **oil of joy instead of mourning** - The oil of joy represents the anointing of the Spirit instead of the tears shed by the mourners. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **a mantle of praise in place of a spirit of dullness** - The mantle of praise is being compared to a glad heart that is filled with praise instead of a heavy heart. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **oaks of righteousness** - This literally describes the strength of the people. They are like trees planted by Yahweh that grow in their righteousness and become strong and give glory to Yahweh.
