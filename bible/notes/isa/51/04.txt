===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **and I will make my justice to be a light for the nations** - Yahweh is comparing his justice to a light. The right things that Yahweh will do will be like a light for the people of all the nations. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **and my arm will judge the nations** - "arm" is a shorter way of referring to Yahweh's power. With power Yahweh will rule over all the nations. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **and for my arm they will eagerly wait** - This is also referring to the power of Yahweh. The people eagerly wait for Yahweh to help them. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
