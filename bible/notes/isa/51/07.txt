===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **For the moth will eat them up like a garment, and the worm will eat them like wool** - This is comparing destruction of the people who taunt them to moths that eat up clothes and worms that eat up wool garments. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
