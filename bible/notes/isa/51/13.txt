===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Why have you forgotten Yahweh your Maker, who stretched out the heavens and laid the foundations of the earth?** - This is a rhetorical question meant to emphasize that they should not forget Yahweh, the one who created their nation, stretched out the skies and laid the foundations of the earth. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
    * **Where is the fury of the oppressor?** - This is also a question meant to emphasize that they should not be afraid of their oppressor because they too have disappeared. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
