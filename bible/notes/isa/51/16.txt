===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I have placed my words in your mouth** - It is with a mouth that words can be proclaimed. Words in my mouth refers to the message given to them by Yahweh to proclaim. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **and I have covered you in the shadow of my hand** - "Hand" refers to Yahweh's protection of them. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
