===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **for the heavens will vanish away like smoke** - This is comparing the disappearing of the skies to a smoke. Someday the skies will disappear like smoke that disappears after a while. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **and the earth will wear out like a garment** - This is comparing the wearing out of the earth to that of old clothes that are worn out. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
