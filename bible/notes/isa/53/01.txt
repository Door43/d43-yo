===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who would have believed what we heard?** - Here "we" is referring to the believing remnant in Israel and Isaiah. "Only a few people will believe what we will tell them about God's servant" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) and (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **And the arm of Yahweh, to whom was it revealed?** - "And Yahweh will reveal his power to them" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **For he grew up before Yahweh like a sapling, and like a sprout out of parched earth** - Here "he" is referring to God's servant who is being compared to a very young tree. See note Isaiah 42:1 for translating "God's servant". AT "For his servant grew up while Yahweh watched, fragile like a very young tree, like a weak young plant that shoots up, a stem that is growing in dry ground." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **he had no remarkable appearance or splendor; when we saw him, there was no beauty to attract us** - "There was nothing majestic about his appearance, no beauty that would draw attention to himself"
