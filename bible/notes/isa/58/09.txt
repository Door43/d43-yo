===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **then your light will rise in the darkness** - This is comparing the good deeds of the people, like feeding the hungry and taking care of the needy to a light that shines in the dark. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **and your darkness will be like the noonday**  - Here Yahweh further compares that if they replace the evil deeds with good deeds they will be like the sun shining at noontime. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
