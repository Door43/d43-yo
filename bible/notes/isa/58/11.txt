===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **You will be like a watered garden** - Yahweh says that if they do everything that he has commanded them, then they will be strong and healthy just like a well watered garden. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **and like a spring of water, whose waters never fail** - This is also a simile saying the same thing as above. If they obey Yahweh's commands, they will be like a spring that never dries up. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
