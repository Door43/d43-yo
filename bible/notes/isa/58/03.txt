===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Why have we fasted," they say, "but you do not see it?** - With this rhetorical question, the Israelite people are defending themselves by saying that they had fasted to please Yahweh, but he did not notice it. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Why have we humbled ourselves, but you do not notice?** - This is also a rhetorical question asked by the Israelite people to decent themselves. They say that they have humbled themselves before Yahweh, but he did not pay attention to it. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
