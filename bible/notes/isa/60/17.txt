===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **but you will call your walls Salvation, and your gates Praise.** - The wall represents safety. They will be safe in their land. And the gates represents the people that live within the city. The people will feel sing praise to Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
