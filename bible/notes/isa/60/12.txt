===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The glory of Lebanon will come to you, the cypress tree, the fir, and the pine together, to beautify my sanctuary** - "The beautiful things in Lebanon will be brought to you — lumber from cypress trees and fir trees and pine trees — to be used to enable my temple to be beautiful."
    * **and I will glorify the place of my feet** - "Place of my feet" represents the temple of Yahweh. Yahweh will make the temple glorious.
