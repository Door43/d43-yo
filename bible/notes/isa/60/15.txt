===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **there will be no one to pass through you** - "no one will ignore you"
    * **You will also drink the milk of the nations, and will nurse at the breast of kings** - "Milk" is being compared to the wealth that the kings of all the other nations will gladly bring. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
