===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the rams of Nebaioth will serve your needs** - Here the rams are talked about as if they can serve people. It means that the rams will be used as a sacrifice on the alters. It can be translated as, "They will bring rams from Nebaioth for you to sacrifice on my altars." (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
