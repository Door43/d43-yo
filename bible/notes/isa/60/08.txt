===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Who are these who fly along like a cloud** - The ships bringing back the people are being compared to a cloud that moves quickly. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **and like the doves to their shelters** - This is also comparing the ships bringing back the people to doves that quickly go to their shelter. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
