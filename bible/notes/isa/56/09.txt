===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All you wild beasts of the field, come and devour, all you beasts in the forest** - This is comparing the armies of the surrounding nations to the animals in the forests. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they are all dumb dogs** - This is comparing the leaders of Israel to dogs that cannot bark. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
