===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **people tall and smooth, from a people feared far and near, a nation mighty and conquering
 whose land the rivers divide** - See how you translated similar phrases in [[en:bible:notes:isa:18:01|18:2]].
  * **to the place of the name of Yahweh of Hosts, to Mount Zion** - The word "name" refers to Yahweh. Alternate translation: "to Mount Zion where Yahweh of Hosts dwells." (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
