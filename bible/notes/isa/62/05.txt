===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **As a young man marries a young woman, so your sons will marry you** - Just like a young married man lives with his wife, the people will live in Judah. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **As a bridegroom rejoices over his bride, your God will rejoice over you** - Just like a bridegroom is happy that his wife belongs to him, Yahweh will be happy that his people now belong to him. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
