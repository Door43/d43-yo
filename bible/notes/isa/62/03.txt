===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **You will also be a crown of beauty in the hand of Yahweh** - Here 'hand' represents the authority of Yahweh. Under his authority they will be like a crown worn by a king. (See: Metonymy)
    * **and your land will be married** - Here the metaphor of "land will be married" is meant to express that Yahweh is so delighted with them, that it will be as though they were married to him. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
