===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **ends of the earth** - "people of every nation"
    * **the daughter of Zion** - The "daughter” of a city means the people of the city. AT: "the people of Israel.” or "the people who live in Jerusalem."
    * **the holy people** - "Yahweh's own people"
