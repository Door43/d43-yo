=== (old content) ===


This is a growing list of 'standard notes' that occur frequently in Isaiah. Please use these so that there are consistent notes about the same topic throughout the book.


**Holy One** - See how you translated this in 1:4.

**the Holy One of Israel** - See how you translated this in 5:16.

**thorns and briers** - See how you translated this in 5:6.

**Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in 1:24.

**the daughter of [name of city]** - The ”daughter” of a city means the people of the city. Alternate translations:  "the people of [name of city]” or ”the people who live in [name of city]."

**the daughter of Zion** - See how you translated this in 10:32.

**holy mountain** - See how you translated this in 11:9.

**declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 14:22. (You can also use this for declaration of Yahweh of Hosts. Just change the part in bold)

**the foundations of the earth** - See how you translated this in 24:18.
