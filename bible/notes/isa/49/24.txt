===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Can the spoils be taken from the warrior, or captives be rescued from a tyrant?** - These rhetorical questions are used to explain that there is no one who can take the valuable things from a soldier who has captured those things in a war and there is no one who can force a tyrant to free the people whom he has captured. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
