===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **desolate** - "abandoned"
    * **The children born during the time of your bereavement will say in your hearing** - Here the time of exile of the Israelites to Babylon is being compared to the time of "bereavement" which means "loss of a loved one". (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
