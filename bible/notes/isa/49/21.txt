===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Who has borne these children for me?** - This rhetorical question is used to express their surprise that so many children belonged to them. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Who has raised these children? ** - This rhetorical question is also used to express deep surprise that even though most of their children were dead and the rest were exiled, they still have so many children and they don't know where they have come from. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
