Yahweh continues speaking.
  * **Turn to me and be saved** - AT: "Worship me and I will save you" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **To me every knee will bend, every tongue will swear** - AT: "Every person will kneel before me, and everyone will swear" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
