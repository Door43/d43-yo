===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will not take**  - "Do not take"
  * **a wife from the Canaanite women **- "from the daughters of Canaan"
  * **Arise, go **- "Go right away"
  * **Paddan Aram**  - See how this was translated in [[:yo:bible:notes:gen:25:19|25:20]].
  * **Bethuel**  - See how you translated this man's name in [[:yo:bible:notes:gen:22:20|22:22]].
  * **your mother’s father**  - "your grandfather"
  * **one of the daughters**  - "from the daughters"
  * **your mother’s brother**  - "your uncle"
