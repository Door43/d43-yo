===== Translation Notes: =====

Peter continues his speech to the Jews that he began in [[:yo:bible:notes:act:01:15|1:16]].

  * **I saw**  - David foresaw God working in his life before events unfolded
  * **before my face**  - in my presence, with me
  * **beside my right hand**  - the right hand was considered the strongest. Someone at the right hand was either the strongest servant, or strongest helper.
  * **my heart was glad and my tongue rejoiced**  - outward expression was given of inward joy
  * **my flesh shall dwell in hope**  - "I will hope in God as long as I live"
