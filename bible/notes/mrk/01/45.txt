===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he went...he spread** - "the man went out...the man spread"
  * **tell everyone and...spread the word** - "began telling many people" (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])

  * **from everywhere ** - "from all over the region" (UDB)
