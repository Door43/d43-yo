===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Truly I say to you."** - See how you translated this in [[:yo:bible:notes:mrk:11:22|11:23]].
  * **the offering box** - This box was the  collection box for the temple offering for all the people.
  * **abundance** - "plenty"
  * **poverty**  - "lack" or "poorness"
