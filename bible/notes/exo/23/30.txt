===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **little by little**  - This refers to a gradual removal of the enemies over a period of time. AT: "a short distance at a time" or "one small group at a time"
  * **become fruitful **- "have more children"
  * **fix your borders**  - "give you the land" or "make the boundaries of your country"
  * **inhabitants**  - "people who live there"
  * **before yourselves **- "in front of your advance" or "ahead of you"
  * **they would make you sin**  - This means that living together with the land's inhabitants would lead to acceptance of their religion and customs.
