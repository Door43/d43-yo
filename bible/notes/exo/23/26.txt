===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **barren **- "infertile" or "unable to have a baby"
  * **miscarry her young**  - AT: "go into labor and deliver a baby too early for it to live"
  * **give you long lives** - AT: "make you live a long time" 
  * **I will send fear of myself on those into whose land you advance** - AT: "I will make the people who now live in the place where you are entering very afraid of me" 
  * **turn their backs to you in fright** - AT: "turn around and run away from you because they are afraid of you"
  * **hornets** - A hornet is a flying insect that can sting people and cause pain, so people are afraid of them.  AT: "terror" or "fear" 
  * **drive out** - "expel" or "remove" 
  * **from before you** - "from your path" or "away from where you are going"
  * **become abandoned** - "left empty"
