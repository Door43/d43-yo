===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Call this day to mind**  - AT: "Remember and celebrate this day" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]] and [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **house of slavery**  - AT: "place where you were slaves"
  * **Yahweh's strong hand**  - The word "hand" refers to power. (See: Metonymy)
  * **No bread with yeast may be eaten**  - AT: "You must not eat bread with yeast" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **Abib**  - This is the name of a month. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **a land flowing with milk and honey**  - See how you translated this in [[:yo:bible:notes:exo:03:07|3:8]].
  * **you must observe this act of worship**  - This sentence introduces what the people must do each year to worship God by remembering this day. AT: "you must worship me by doing this" or "you must have this festival."
