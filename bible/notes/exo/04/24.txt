===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh met Moses and tried to kill him** - AT: "Yahweh met Moses and tried to cause him to die"; Possible meaning: Yahweh wanted to kill Moses because he had not circumcised his son.
  * **Zipporah** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **flint knife** - a knife with a sharpened stone blade
  * **Moses' feet** - This may be a milder or more polite way to refer to Moses' genitals. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **You are a bridegroom of blood** - AT: "You are now related to me by blood"
