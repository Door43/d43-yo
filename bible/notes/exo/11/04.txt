===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **midnight** - This is the time of 12 am (at night) or 2400 hours.
  * **firstborn** - the child who is born first in a family
  * **the slave girl behind the hand mill** - This means the person who grinds grain. Grain is a small, hard seed from a plant used for food (such as wheat, corn, and rice).
  * **cattle** - These are animals raised for food, including large and small animals.
