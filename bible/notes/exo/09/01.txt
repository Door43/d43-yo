===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues talking with Moses about dealing with Pharaoh and bringing the Hebrew people out of Egypt.
  * **if you refuse to let them go, if you still keep them back** - AT: "If you still refuse to let them go" (UDB); (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **then Yahweh's hand will be on your cattle** - Yahweh will show his power by sending a terrible disease upon the Egyptians' livestock. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
