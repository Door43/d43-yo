===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you must set up** - Here "you" refers to Moses, but God means for Moses to tell the people to set up the tabernacle. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **tabernacle, the tent of meeting** - These refer to the same thing.
