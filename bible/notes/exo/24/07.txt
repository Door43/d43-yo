===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **book of the covenant** - This is the book or scroll that Moses wrote down in verse 4 of what God commanded his people to do.   
  * **aloud**  - "out loud" or “in a loud voice”
  * **took the blood** - AT: "took the blood that was in the bowls"
  * **the blood of the covenant that Yahweh has made with you by giving you this promise with all these words** - AT: "the blood that confirms the agreement Yahweh made with you when he gave you all these commands"
