===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The appearance of Yahweh's glory**  - This refers to how Yahweh looked–what they saw–when they looked at him. AT: "Yahweh's glory looked"
  * **like a devouring fire**  - This is a comparison. The face of God looked like a great fire. (See: Simile.)
