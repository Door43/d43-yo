===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Israel's elders**  - This refers to men of prominence in the nation, heads of household and other leaders. The word "elder" means "older person" in its basic meaning.
  * **pavement**  - A hard surface for walking, riding, and driving. It is usually made of stone or concrete such as the type used for roads or plazas.
  * **sapphire stone **- This refers to a gemstone that is blue in color.
  * **as clear as the sky**  - This sapphire pavement was blue and reflective, with no imperfections, like a sky with no clouds. (See: Simile)
  * **lay a hand in anger**  - This is an idiom that means "to hit because of anger." (See: Idiom.)
