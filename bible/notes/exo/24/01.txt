===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Aaron, Nadab, Abihu** - These men were other leaders who served under Moses along with the seventy elders.
  * **at a distance** - The leaders were not to come too close to Yahweh.  
  * **Moses alone** - "only Moses"
  * **The others must not come near, nor may the people come up with him** - The leaders of the Israelites and the rest of the Israelite people may not come up to God with Moses
