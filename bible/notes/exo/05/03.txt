===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **God of the Hebrews** - a term also used for the Israelites' God or Yahweh
  * **so that he does not attack us with plague** - AT: "so that he does not cause us to die from disease" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **with the sword** - AT: "attacks by our enemies" (UDB); (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **why are you taking the people from their work?** - This question is used to emphasize that the Israelites should not be taken away from their work. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
