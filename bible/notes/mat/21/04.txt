===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus riding a donkey into Jerusalem.

  * **that there might be fulfilled that which had been spoken through the prophet** - "God had said this many years ahead of time by his prophet that this was going to happen." (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that which had been spoken through the prophet** - "that which a prophet had told before it happened." (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **daughter of Zion** - Israel (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **donkey** - an animal ridden by poor people
  * **colt** - a young male donkey
