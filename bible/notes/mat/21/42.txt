===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus uses the prophets to explain the parable.

  * **Jesus said to them** - "Jesus said to the chief priests and elders"
  * **'The stone which the builders rejected, has been made the cornerstone.** - "The stone that was rejected by the builders became the most important one." (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **This was from the Lord** - "The Lord has caused this great change"
