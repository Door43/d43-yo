===== Translation Notes: =====

The religious leaders react to the parable that Jesus told.

  * **his parables** - "Jesus' parables"
  * **they saw that he was speaking.** - the chief priests and Pharisees saw that Jesus was speaking.
  * **to lay hands on him** - "to arrest Jesus"
