===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the reaction of the authorities to the resurrection.

  * **the governor**  - Pilate was the local ruler for the Roman Caesar. (See: Metonymy)
  * **we will convince him **- the priests and elders will convince the governor
  * **take any worries away from you**  - "take any fears and concerns from you" (away from the soldiers)
  * **as they had been instructed **- "as the priests instructed them" (See" Active/Passive)
  * **continues even today**  - This continued even until the time Matthew wrote this book.
