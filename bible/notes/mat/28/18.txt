===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus meeting with his disciples after his resurrection.

  * **came to them and spoke to them**  - came to the disciples and spoke to the disciples
  * **given to me**  - given to Jesus
  * **All authority has been given to me**  - "God has given all authority to me" (See: Active or Passive)
  * **Baptize them**  - "Baptize these disciples"
