===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the resurrection of Jesus from the dead.

  * **The women **- "Mary Magdalene and the other Mary"
  * **behold** - standard note
  * **Jesus met them**  - "Jesus met Mary Magdalene and the other Mary"
  * **All hail**  -** "**Peace be to you!" (UDB)
  * **​took hold of his feet **-** "**knelt down and held his feet"
  * **my brothers **- Jesus' disciples
  * **they will see me**  - Jesus' disciples will see Jesus
