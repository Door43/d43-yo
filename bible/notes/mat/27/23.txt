===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.

  * **Why, what crime has he done **- The word "he" refers to Jesus.
  * **they**  - The word "they" refers to the crowd of people.
  * **he took water, washed his hands **- This was a symbolic action. By washing his hands, Pilate may have believed he was transferring responsibility for the judgment from himself to the crowd. 
  * **the blood**  - "the death" (See: Metonymy)
