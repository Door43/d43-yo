===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.

  * **So when they were gathered together**  - "So when the crowd had gathered"
  * **Who do you want me to release for you**  - Because of his custom each Passover, Pilate is asking the Pharisees and people gathered there whom they want him to release from prison.
  * **handed him over**  - The word "him" refers to Jesus.
  * **handed him over** - "given him over" or "brought Jesus to him"
  * **While he was sitting**  - The word "he" refers to Pilate.
  * **sent word**  - "sent a message"
