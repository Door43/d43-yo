===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.

  * **the feast**  - This use of the word "feast" refers to the Feast of Unleavened Bread, during which the Passover was celebrated.
  * **prisoner chosen by the crowd** - "prisoner whom the crowd had chosen" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **notorious**  - The word "notorious" means well-known for doing something bad.
