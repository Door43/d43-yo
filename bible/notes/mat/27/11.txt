===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.

  * **the governor **- The governor is Pilate, a Roman leader.
  * **You say so **- "It is as you say."
  * **But when he was accused by the chief priests and elders**  - "But when the chief priests and elders accused him" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **Do you not hear all the charges against you?** - Pilate knows that Jesus hears the charges. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **But he did not answer even one word, so that the governor was greatly amazed **- "But Jesus did not answer even one word; this greatly amazed the governor." (See: Active/Passive)
