===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins the account of the Roman soldiers mocking Jesus.

  * **the Praetorium**  - "The Praetorium" refers to the governor's residence or headquarters.
  * **stripped him**  - "pulled off his clothes"
  * **a scarlet robe**  - The word "scarlet" is a dark red color.
  * **Hail, King of the Jews**  - As used here, "hail" is a cheer or greeting to a person of honor. Alternate translation: "Praise you, King of the Jews" or "Praise to the King of the Jews"
