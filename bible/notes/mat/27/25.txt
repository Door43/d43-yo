===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.

  * **his blood**- "his death" (See: Metonymy)
  * **May his blood be on us and our children** - "May we and our children be held responsible for his death"
  * **Then he released Barabbas**  - The word "he" refers to Pilate, who ordered his soldiers to release Barabbas.
  * **but he scourged Jesus** - The word "he" refers to Pilate, who ordered his soldiers to scourge Jesus.
  * **scourged** - The word "scourged" means that the soldiers whipped Jesus' back many times.
