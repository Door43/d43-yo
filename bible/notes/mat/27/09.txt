===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how Judas killed himself.

  * **Then that which had been spoken by Jeremiah the prophet was fulfilled, saying** - "The prophet Jeremiah spoke this prophecy, and it had come true; he had said" (See Active/Passive)
  * **people of Israel **  - "People of Israel" means the Israelite religious leaders. (See: Metonymy)
  * **the potter's field **  - The Old Testament book of Jeremiah records that the prophet bought a field, and this probably refers to that field.
  * **directed me**  - The word "me" refers to Jeremiah the prophet, who also had bought a field in the Old Testament book of Jeremiah.
