===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the crucifixion and death of Jesus.

  * **Two robbers were crucified with him**  - "The soldiers crucified two robbers with Jesus" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **insulted him**  - "insulted Jesus"
  * **shaking their heads**  - By shaking their heads they are mocking Jesus.
  * **You who would destroy the temple **- The people say this because Jesus had said earlier that if people destroyed the temple, he would raise it up in three days. (See: John 2:19)
