===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples as he eats the Passover meal with them.

  * **blessed it** - "gave thanks for it" or "thanked God for it" (UDB).
  * **This is my body **- Possible meanings are 1) “This bread is my body” and 2) “This bread represents my body.” (See: Metaphor)
