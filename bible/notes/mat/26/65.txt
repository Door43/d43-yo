===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus being questioned by the high priest.

  * **the high priest tore his clothes**  - tearing an outer coat or cloak was a sign of anger or grief in the case of blasphemy
  * **Why do we still need witnesses**  - This is a rhetorical question that can be made a statement as in the UDB. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **witnesses **- These are persons who witness or testify.
  * **They answered and said**  - "The Jewish leaders answered and said"
