===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins the account of the arrest of Jesus in the garden of Gethsemane.

  * **he came up to Jesus **- "Judas came up to Jesus"
  * **and kissed him **- "and greeted him with a kiss"
  * **they came, laid hands on Jesus, and seized him **- "Laid hands on him" is a figure of speech that means they came and took Jesus by force to arrest him. (See: Idiom)
