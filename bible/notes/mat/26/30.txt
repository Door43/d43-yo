===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples as they walk to the Mount of Olives.

  * **hymn **- A hymn is a type of song. It was traditional for them to sing an Old Testament Psalm at this point.
  * **fall away **- "run away" or "abandon me"
  * **for it is written** - Here Jesus quotes Zechariah the prophet in the Old Testament.
  * **strike the shepherd** - "strike down the shepherd" or "cause men to kill the shepherd" (UDB) (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metaphor]])
  * **sheep of the flock will be scattered **- "sheep of the flock will scatter" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **sheep of the flock** - This refers to the disciples who would forsake Jesus. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metaphor]])
  * **after I am raised up** - "after God raises me up"  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **raised up** - In this context, "raised up" means being raised back to life from the dead.
