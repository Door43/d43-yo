===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus praying in the garden of Gethsemane.

  * **Are you still sleeping and taking your rest **- This is a rhetorical question that could be written as a statement as in the UDB. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **the hour is at hand**  - This is a figure of speech that means "the time has come."
  * **is being betrayed into the hands of sinners **- This can be written as an active phrase as in the UDB.
  * **into the hands of sinners**  - "handed over to sinful people" (See: Synecdoche)
