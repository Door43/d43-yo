===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how Peter denies he knows Jesus.

  * **one of them **- "one of those who were with Jesus"
  * **for your accent gives you away **- "for we can tell from your accent"
  * **accent **- This refers to small differences in speech that are usually regional variations.
  * **your accent **- "the way you talk" or "your Galilean accent"
  * **curse **- "call down a curse on himself"
  * **swear **- This would be similar to the oath in Peter's second denial (see UDB).
  * **"I do not know the man"**  - This can be expressed indirectly as "that he did not know the man". (See: Direct or Indirect)
  * **wept bitterly**  - This means he wept with great sorrow for what he had done (see UDB).
