===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus praises the woman who anointed himÂ before his death.

  * **But Jesus knowing this ** - Jesus knew what the disciples were thinking and saying.
  * **Why are you troubling this woman** - Jesus is not expecting them to answer this question. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **You always have the poor with you, but you will not always have me **- The word "you" here refers to the disciples.
