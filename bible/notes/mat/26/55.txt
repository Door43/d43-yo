===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the arrest of Jesus in the garden of Gethsemane.

  * **Have you come out with swords and clubs to seize me like a robber **- This is a rhetorical question that can be translated as a statement if necessary, such as "You know that I am not a robber, yet you come out to me bringing swords and clubs." (See: Rhetorical Question**)**
  * **all this has happened so that the writings of the prophets might be fulfilled**  - This passive phrase can be written in active form as in the UDB. (See: Active or Passive)
  * **left him **- "abandoned him" or "deserted him" (UDB)
