===== Translation Notes: =====

A woman anoints JesusÂ before his death.

  * **Simon the leper ** - This refers to a man named Simon who had suffered from a dreaded skin disease but who had apparently been healed by this time.
  * **a woman came to him **- The word "him" refers to Jesus, not Simon the leper.
  * **alabaster jar**- An "alabaster jar" is a container made of alabaster, which is a soft stone having a creamy color.
  * **ointment** - This refers to a perfume or an oil that has a pleasing odor.
  * **What is the reason for this waste** - The disciples were not asking for an answer. They were actually complaining that she did that. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **for this waste** - "for uselessly pouring it out" or "uselessly wasting it"
