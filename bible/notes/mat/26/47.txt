===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus praying in the garden of Gethsemane.

  * **While he was still speaking **- "While Jesus was still speaking"
  * **one of the twelve **- "one of the twelve disciples"
  * **chief priests **- See definition of Important Term "priest" for the different kinds of Jewish priests.
  * **Now the betrayer had given them a sign, saying, "Whomever I kiss, that is he; seize him."**  - If necessary, this can be translated as an indirect sentence such as "Now the betrayer had given them as sign, telling them to seize the one he kissed."
  * **sign **- In this verse, "sign" means "signal."
  * **Whomever I kiss**  - "The one I will kiss" or "The man whom I will kiss" (UDB)
  * **kiss **- This would have normally been a respectful kiss of greeting.
