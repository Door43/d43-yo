===== Translation Notes: =====

The Jewish leaders plot to have Jesus arrested and killed.

  * **palace ** - The word "palace" is a large, expensive house. 
  * **stealthily ** - "by deceit" or "secretly"
  * **during the feast** - The yearly passover feast was about to begin.
  * **a riot does not arise among the people ** - "a riot does not happen among the people"
