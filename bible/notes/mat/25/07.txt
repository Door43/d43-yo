===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell a parable about wise and foolish virgins.

  * **trimmed their lamps **- "adjusted their lamps for burning"
  * **The foolish said to the wise**  - "The foolish virgins said to the wise virgins"
  * **our lamps are going out ** - “the fire in our lamps is dying out” or “our torches are no longer burning brightly” (See: Idiom)
