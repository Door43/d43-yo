===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell a parable about wise and foolish virgins.

  * **the bridegroom was delayed** - "something delayed the bridegroom" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **they ** - The word "they" refers to all ten virgins.
