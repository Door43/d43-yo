===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus begins to tell his disciples how he will judge people at the end of the age.

  * **sit on his glorious throne**  - "sit on the throne of his glory" or "sit on the throne of his praise"
  * **Before him will be gathered all the nations**  - "He will gather all the nations before him." (See: Active/Passive)
  * **Before him** - "In front of him"
  * **all the nations **- "all people from every country" (See: Metonymy)
  * **as a shepherd separates the sheep from the goats** - He distinguishes two distinct groups. (See: Simile)
  * **goats - **  Goats are medium-sized four-footed mammals that are similar to sheep, often domesticated or herded like sheep.
  * **He will place** - "He" refers to the Son of Man.
