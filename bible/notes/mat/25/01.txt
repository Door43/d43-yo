===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus begins to tell a parable about wise and foolish virgins.

  * **Then the kingdom of heaven will be like **- This begins a parable. Jesus compares this story about the wise and foolish virgins to his coming again to earth with his kingdom. (See: Parable)
  * **lamps **- These could have been either oil lamps or torches. (A torch was made by wrapping some kind of cloth onto the end of a stick or pole and soaking the cloth with oil. When the torch or lamp was lit, it would burn until the oil was gone.)
  * **Five of them **- "Five of the virgins"
  * **containers of oil**  - "jars of oil"
