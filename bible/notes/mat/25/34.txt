===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples how he will judge people at the end of the age.

  * **the King**- Jesus was referring here to himself. He is the Son of Man who was described in the previous verses as sitting on his glorious throne in judgment.
  * **those on his right hand** - "those people at his right hand."  This is those to whom he referred to as sheep.
  * **Come, you who have been blessed by my Father ** - "Come, you who my Father has blessed" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **inherit the kingdom prepared for you**  - "inherit the kingdom that God prepared for you"
  * **inherit ** - "receive" or "possess" or "be an heir of"
  * **from the foundation of the world ** -  "from the time the world was created" (See: Metonymy) or "since the time he created the world" (UDB).
