===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * **do not believe it**  - "it" refers to the false things they have said to you.
  * **false**  - things that are not true
  * **elect**  - "select" or "chosen"
  * **I have told you in advance**  - "I have told you before it happens"
