===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * Jesus continues to talk with his disciples on the Mount of Olives about the end times.
  * **Because iniquity will be multiplied **- Because people will sin more" (See: Active/Passive and Idiom)
  * **the love of many will grow cold**  - "Many people will begin to love God less." or "Many people will no longer love God." (See: Active/Passive and Idiom)
  * **will be saved **- This passive expression can be translated as "God will save them."
  * **the gospel of the kingdom will be preached **- People will preach the gospel of the kingdom. (See: Active/Passive)
  * **the end will come.**  - "God will bring the end." (See: Active/Passive)
