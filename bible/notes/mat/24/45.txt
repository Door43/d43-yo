===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * **So who is the faithful, wise servant…everything that he owns**  - Jesus is telling a parable about his return to earth.
  * **So who is the faithful, wise servant … **- Jesus is asking people to think about who is a faithful servant (those who are doing their jobs as servants). (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **them **- "them" refers to the people in the master's home.
