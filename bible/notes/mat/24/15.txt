===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * **abomination**  - This means something one hates and considers very bad.
  * **desolation**  - This means ruin, devastation. ​
  * **when you see the abomination of desolation **- This probably refers to something that is religiously unclean and a symbol of destroying God's name.
  * **which was spoken of by Daniel the prophet **- written about by the Old Testament prophet Daniel (9:27; 11:31). This can be translated as "which Daniel, the Old Testament prophet, wrote about (9:27, 11:31). (See: Active/Passive)
  * **standing in the holy place **  - This means that the abomination of desolation, either a very bad person or a statute representing him, will be set up in the holy place.
  * **the holy place**  - This refers to: 1) the part of the Jewish temple that was set apart for special offerings; or 2) a place that the people considered special for God's service.
  * **housetop**  - This refers to the roof, which at that time was usually flat with an outside stairway; a place to rest and relax.
  * **cloak**  - "coat​"
