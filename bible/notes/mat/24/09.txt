===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * Jesus continues his talk with the disciples on the Mount of Olives about the end times.
  * **Then they will deliver you up **- "they" refers to the people that are persecuting the believers.
  * **they will deliver you up to tribulation**  - "they will cause you trouble" or "they will torture you"
  * **you**  - followers of Jesus
  * **You will be hated by all the nations **- "All the people of the nations will hate you." (See: Active/Passive) (See: Metonymy)
  * **many will stumble and betray one another** - "Many people will turn away and betray each other,"
  * **rise up**  - "come" (UDB) or "appear"
