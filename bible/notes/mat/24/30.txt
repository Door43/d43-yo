===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * **beat their breasts**  - They beat their chests. This is an expression of great fear or worry because of punishment that was coming.
  * **trumpet **- " a metal wind instrument"
  * **they will gather**  - //They//  refers to his angels.
  * **his elect**  - the Son of Man's select or chosen people
  * **the four winds**  - This refers to the four quarters or corners of the earth. This could be translated as "from everywhere." (See UDB) (See: Metonymy)
  * **from one end of the sky to the other**  - (See: doublet)
