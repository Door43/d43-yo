===== Translation Notes: =====

A man named Huram came from Tyre to make bronze items for the temple.

    * **sent for** - "invited" (UDB)
    * **brought him from Tyre** - This tells us that Huram accepted the invitation and came to Jerusalem. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **widow** - a woman who lost her husband by death and has not married again
    * **Huram was the son of a widow** and **his father was a man of Tyre** - Because Huram's mother was a widow, we know that his father was dead.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
    * **Huram was filled with wisdom and understanding and skill** - We can assume that God filled Huram with wisdom, understanding and skill. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) AT: "Huram was very wise, intelligent and skilled"
