===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues to list items for the temple that Huram made of bronze.

  * **He**  - He refers to Huram and his helpers. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **the sea**  - see :23
  * **under it**  - Place a semicolon after this phrase to separate items in the list. AT: "under it;"
  * **also the pots,**  - AT: "the pots,"
  * **implements**  - tools
  * **Huram made them for King Solomon, for the temple of Yahweh, of burnished bronze.**  AT: "Huram and his workers made all those things of polished bronze for King Solomon for the temple."
  * **them/those**  - Them and those refer to the items listed that Huram had finished making. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns|English Plural Pronouns]])
