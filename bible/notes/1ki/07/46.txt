===== Translation Notes: =====

The author is writing about making bronze items for the temple.

  * **cast**  - see :23
  * **The king had cast them**  - It is implicit that the king did not do the work, but that his workers did it. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]].)
  * **plain of the Jordan,**  - This refers to the flat land near the Jordan River.
  * **in the clay ground between Succoth and Zarethan.**  - Succoth and Zarethan were cities in the Jordan River Valley. This is where Huram made clay molds for items to be cast of bronze. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Solomon did not weigh all the implements**  - It is implicit that the King himself would not do the weighing, but also that his workers did not weigh the items. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]].)
  * **implements**  - tools or the bronze items that Huram made
  * **so the weight of the bronze could not be known**  - (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]]) AT: "so no one knows the weight of the bronze objects."
