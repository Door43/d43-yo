===== Translation Notes: =====

Huram made 2 bronze pillars for the temple.

  * **cubit**  - (See: [[:yo:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **circumference**  - the distance or measurement around something
  * **capital**  - In this verse, capital refers to a decoration on top of the column or pillar.
  * **burnished bronze**  - the bronze was polished and shiny
  * **checker latticework**  - "woven pattern of crossed metal strips"
  * **checker latticework and wreaths of chain work**  - AT: "bronze wreaths of chains" (UDB)
