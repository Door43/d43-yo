===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **walk in his ways** - "live the way he commands" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **prosper**  - "succeed" or "do well"
  * ** with all their heart and with all their soul** - These phrases mean the same thing and are used for emphasis. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **you will never cease to have a man on the throne of Israel** - AT: "one of your descendants will always be the king of Israel"; (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
