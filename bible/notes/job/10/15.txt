===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **if I am wicked**  - "if I am guilty"
  * **even if I were righteous **- "if I am innocent"
  * **I could not lift up my head **- AT: "I could not look to you"
  * **since I am filled with disgrace **- The term "disgrace" is referring to Job's shame in his circumstances. AT: "Since I am ashamed"
  * **and am looking at my own suffering **- AT: "and am focused on my situation"
  * **if my head lifts itself, you hunt me down like a lion **- AT: "if I am proud, you hunt me like a lion"
  * **show yourself powerful **- AT: "show your marvelous power" or "show your awesomeness"
