===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My soul is weary of life **- AT: "I am tired of living," or "I hate my life".
  * **My soul **- This is a poetic way of saying "I."
  * **is weary**  - AT: "is disgusted with" or "hates."
  * **I will give free expression of my complaint**  - AT: "I will not hold back my complaint any longer," or "I will tell God exactly how I feel he has wronged me."
  * **I will speak in the bitterness of my soul **- AT: "I will speak out of the anger that I feel," or "I will speak because of the pain I have felt."
  * **Is it good to you that you should oppress me, to despise the work of your hands **  - AT: "Is it good to you that you should oppress, that you should reject the work of your hands"
  * **the work of your hands**  - explanation
  * **Is it good to you that you should oppress … while you smile on the plans of the wicked? **  - This is a question that expects a "no" answer. One could change the question into a statement. AT: "It is not good that you should oppress … while you smile on the plans of the wicked." (See: Rhetorical Question)
