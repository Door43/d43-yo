===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If your children have sinned against him** - "If your children are sinning against God"
  * **then he has given them over to the power their sins** - "it is because God has allowed the power of their sin to control them"
  * **But if you diligently seek God and seek the favor of the Almighty** - Here the writer expresses the single idea of seeking God using two different statements.  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
