===== Translation Notes: =====

  * “**Can you understand God” “Can you comprehend the Almighty”**- This is a poetic figure of speech in which two phrases say the same idea, or explain the same idea using different words (see: [[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol2/translate/figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **"Can you understand God by searching for him?"- AT: "You can not understand God by searching for him." (See: Rhetorical Question )**
  * **"Can you comprehend the Almighty perfectly?" - AT: "You can not comprehend the Almighty perfectly." (See: Rhetorical Question )**
  * **“The matter”**-is referring to the being of God or the essence of God
  * **“high as heaven” “deeper the Sheol” ****“longer than the earth” “wider than the sea” **- These phrases are figures of speech comparing the greatness of God with "as" or "like" to something known or familiar (see: Simile ) (See: [[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol2/translate/figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **"What can you do?" - AT: "you can not do anything about the matter." (See: Rhetorical Question )**
  * **"What can you know?"**  - AT: "You can not know anythng about God." (See: Rhetorical Question )
  * **"its"**  - refers to "the Matter" or the being of God.
