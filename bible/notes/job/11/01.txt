===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Should not a multitude of words be answered **- Zophar is using a rhetorical question in the negative to show that Job's words must be scolded. AT "These words must be answered" (See: Litotes, Rhetorical Question)
  * **should this man, so full of talk, be made legitimate?**  - AT "many words alone do not clear a man"
  * **Should your boasting make others hold their peace**  - AT "Should your many words silence men?"
  * **When you mock our teaching, will no one make you feel ashamed?**  - AT "You scoff and their is no one to shame you"
