===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **answered**  - "responded"
    * **No doubt**  - "Surely,"
    * **you are the people;**  - "you are important people"
    * **“No doubt you are the people; wisdom will die with you.**  - In this figure of speech. Job mocks how they are acting and shows how rediculous they sound.  (see: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]]), ("you" is plural in these next two sentences. See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns]]) “Surely you both are such important people that wisdom cannot exist without you." AT: "Both of you both are acting like you are the only source of wisdom, here."
    * **But I have understanding as well as you; I am not inferior to you.**  - "But, I am not inferior to you both. I understand as well as both of you do.
    * **Indeed, who does not know such things as these?**  - "In fact, who does not understand these things?"
