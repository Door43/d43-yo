===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Sing, daughter of Zion!**  - "Sing, people of Zion (or "Jerusalem")!
  * **Be glad and rejoice with all your heart,**  - See (Doublet) "be glad and rejoice in your inner being,"
  * **they will say to Jerusalem, **- "they will say to the people of Jerusalem,"
  * **Do not let your hands falter.**  - See ( Idiom) AT: "Do not become weak or discouraged." See (UDB) or "Let Yahweh keep you strong."
