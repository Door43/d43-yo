===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Her princes are roaring lions in her midst!**  - See (Metaphor) AT: "Jerusalem's leaders look for ways they can get personal gain with lies and cheating!"
  * **Her judges are evening wolves**  - See (Metaphor) AT: "Her judges are vicious and prey on the people," or "Her judges are vicious and take advantage of the people,"
  * **who leave nothing to be gnawed upon in the morning!**  - See (Metaphor) AT: "taking everything from the people they prey upon!" or"taking everything from the people they are cheating."
  * **Her prophets are insolent and treasonous men!**  - See (Hendiadys) AT: "Her prophets are proud liars that seek their own gain.
  * **Her priests have profaned what is holy**  - AT: "Her priests have sinned in the temple"
  * **and have done violence to the Law!**  - AT: "by disobeying and distorting God's instructions."
