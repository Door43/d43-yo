===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **city/she/her**  - These all refer to Jerusalem in v1-2.
  * **Woe to the rebellious city! **- AT: "What sorrow awaits the disobedient city of Jerulsalem."
  * **The violent city is defiled!**  - AT: "The people have become violent (or "brutal" or "corrupt") and their sin is unacceptable to God."
  * **She has not listened to the voice of God,**  - See (Synecdoche) AT: "She has not listened (or "paid attention") to the messages from God,"
  * **She does not trust in Yahweh **- AT: "She has withdrawn her faith in Yahweh"
  * **and will not draw near to her God.**  - See (Metonomy) AT: "and refuses to repent and trust her God."
