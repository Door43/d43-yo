===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **deal with all your oppressors; **- AT: "deal severely with all who have hurt and mistreated you;" or "deal severely with all your enemies,"
  * **I will gather you together and bring you back home to Israel.**  - AT: "I will gather you together at home in Israel."
  * **for I will give you a name and glory among all the peoples of the earth**  - AT: "You will be respected and greatly praised wherever you were mocked and shamed".
  * **when I restore your fortunes before your eyes,"**  - AT: "when they see how you prosper,"
