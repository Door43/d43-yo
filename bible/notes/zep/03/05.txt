===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh is righteous in her midst!**  - AT: "But Yahweh is still in Jerusalem and can be trusted! (or "does no wrong!")"
  * **He can do no wrong!**  - AT: "In fact, he cannot do wrong because he is righteous!"
  * **Morning by morning he will dispense his justice! **- AT: "He will treat people fairly, day after day," See (UDB)
  * **It**** will not be hidden in the light,**  - AT: "and everybody will know it."
  * **yet criminals know no shame! **- AT: "Wicked people are never ashamed of their sin."
