===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But then I will give pure lips to the people,**  - See (Metonymy). AT: "After that time I will give my people clear minds (or "good thoughts") and their speech will be pure"
  * **to call all of them in the name of Yahweh to serve me standing shoulder to shoulder**  - See (Metonymy). AT: "so that they may all worship me together."
  * **From beyond the river of Ethiopia my worshipers—my scattered people—**  - AT: "Together,my people that were forced out of Israel and are farther away than the river of Ethiopia"
  * **will bring offerings due me**. - AT: "will worship me and present offerings that are due me."
  * **In that day you will not be put to shame for all your deeds that you committed against me,**  - AT: "In that time you will no longer need to be ashamed, for you will no longer be sinning (or "rebelling") against me."
  * **since at that time I will remove from among you those who celebrated your pride,**  - AT; "I will remove the proud and arrogant people from among you,"
  * **and because you will no longer act arrogantly on my holy mountain.**  - AT: "and there will be no one acting arrogantly on my holy mountain." "holy mountain" refers to "Mount Zion" or "Zion".
