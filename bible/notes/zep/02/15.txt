===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is the exultant city that lived without fear,**  - AT: "The people of this proud and boistrous city thought that they were safe (or "secure")."
  * **exultant city**  - The city refered to in v15 (including "she" and "her ") is Ninevah.
  * **that said in her heart, 'I am, and nothing is my equal!**' - AT: "The people said "Our city is the greatest and no other city is as great as ours!" See (UDB)
  * **his and shake his fist **- See (Idiom) "his and shake their fist" is an emotional act of defiance and retaliation. Judah was happy that God had destoyed Assyria because they had plundered Judah earlier.
