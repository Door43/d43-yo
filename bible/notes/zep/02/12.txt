===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You Cushites also**  - AT: "You Cushites of Ethiopia"
  * **will be pierced by my sword,**  - AT: "will be slaughtered, says the Lord,"
  * **and God's hand will attack the north and destroy Assyria,**  - AT: "and I will destroy Assyria to the north,"
  * **so that Nineveh will become an abandoned devastation,**  - AT: "so that Ninevah will be an abandoned and desolate wasteland." "Ninevah" was the capital city of Assyria."
  * **every beast of the nations,**  - "every kind of animal,"
  * **in the tops of her pillars.**  - When buildings were destroyed and fell down, the columns (or pillars) used for decoration and support would often remain standing.
  * **There will be a chirping sound in the windows and ravens will call on their doorsteps, for he has exposed the cedar beams.**  - AT: "Birds will be living and chirping in the ruins. The valuable cedar beams will be exposed."
