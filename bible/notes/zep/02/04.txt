===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **(Ashkelon), (Ashdod), (Ekron), and (Cherethites)**  -See (How to Translate Names)
  * **For Gaza will be abandoned, and Ashkelon will turn into a devastation! **- See (Hendiadys) AT; "Gaza and Ashkelon will be devastated and abandoned (or "vacated")!
  * **They will drive out Ashdod at noon, and they will uproot Ekron! - **  See (Hendiadys) " AT: Ashdod and Ekron will be driven out!"
  * **Woe to the inhabitants of the seacoast, the nation of the Cherethites!**  - AT: "Great sorrow awaits the Philistines in the nation of the Cherethites, who live at the edge of the great sea."
  * **Yahweh has spoken against you, **- "Yahweh has spoken judgment against you,"
  * **until no inhabitant remains!**  - "until no one is left!"
