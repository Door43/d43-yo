===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let every soul be obedient to** - "Every Christian should obey" (see UDB) or "Everyone should obey" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **higher authorities** - "government officials"
  * **for**  - "because"
  * **the ones that exist have been appointed by God** - "the people who are in authority are there because God put them there" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **that authority**  - "that government authority"
  * **those who oppose it**  - "those who oppose government authority"
