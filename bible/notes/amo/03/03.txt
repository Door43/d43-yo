===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Will two walk together unless they have agreed**  - This is a Rhetorical Question to emphasize the absolute truth of what God is going to say later in verses 7-8.
    * **Will a lion roar in the forest when he has no victim**  - This is a Rhetorical Question to emphasize the absolute truth of what God is going to say later in verses 7-8.
    * **Will a young lion growl from his den if he has caught nothing**  - This is a Rhetorical Question to emphasize the absolute truth of what God is going to say later in verses 7-8.
