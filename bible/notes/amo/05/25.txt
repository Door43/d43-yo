===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Did you bring me sacrifices and offerings in the wilderness for forty years, house of Israel?**  - This is a [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]
    * **house of Israel** - or "nation of Israel" or "descendants of Israel" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **You will lift up Sikkuth as your king** - Here "Sikkuth" is a pagan god.  See how the UDB interprets this.
