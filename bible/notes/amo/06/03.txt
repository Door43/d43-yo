===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Woe to those who put off the day of disaster** - Here "put off" means to "shrug off".  See the UDB for clarification.
    * **Woe to those who put off the day of disaster and make the throne of violence come near** - See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]
    * **throne of violence** - Implies a violent enemy kingdom (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **They lie on beds of ivory and lounge on their couches.**  - Implies comfortable living at the expense of introspection and soul-searching.  
    * **They eat lambs from the flock and calves from the stall**  - See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]
