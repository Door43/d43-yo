===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking to the Pharisee who had invited him to his home.)
    * **they cannot repay you**  - This can be translated as "they cannot invite you to a banquet in return."
    * **in the resurrection of the just**  - "when the righteous people are resurrected from the dead"
