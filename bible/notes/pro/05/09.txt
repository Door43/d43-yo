===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **lest**  - The word "lest" is a conjunction used when you are saying something in order to prevent something bad from happening.  AT: "for fear that"
    * **give away years of your life to a cruel person**  - An adulteress is cruel, and following her way will cause great harm to your life.
