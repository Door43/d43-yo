===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **A wise child makes his father rejoice**  - AT: "A wise child does what is right and makes his father rejoice" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
    * **Treasures accumulated by wickedness have no value**  - AT: "Wickedness results in death. Therefore, treasures gained by it have no value" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
    * **Yahweh does not let those who do what is right to go hungry **  - AT: "Yahweh makes sure those who do what is right have food to eat" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
