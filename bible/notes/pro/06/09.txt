===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **How long will you sleep, you lazy person?**  - The father used this question to tell his child to not be lazy. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **When will you rise from your sleep?**  - The father used this question to tell his child to go to work. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **your needs like an armed soldier**  - you will be attacked by neediness like an armed soldier would attack (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
