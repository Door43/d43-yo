===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **it will guide you**  - The word "it" here refers to the lessons taught by the father and mother (verse 20).
    * **the commands are a lamp** - the commands are like a lamp that lights your way (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **the teaching is a light** - the teaching is like a light (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
