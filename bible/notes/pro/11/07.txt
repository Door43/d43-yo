===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his hope perishes**  - "his hope dissappears"
  * **the hope that was in his strength**  - "the confidence he has in his own power"
  * **comes to nothing**  - "dissappears"
  * **it comes**  - "it" refers to trouble
