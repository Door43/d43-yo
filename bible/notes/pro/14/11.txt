===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the tent of the upright** - the household of the upright (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **flourish**  - to do well and last long, or to be healthy, or to be very successful
  * **a way that seems right to a man** - It appears to the person that it is a good way to live.
