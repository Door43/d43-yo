===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **breathes out lies** - speaks lies (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])   
    * **the one who is discerning**  - "the one who is wise" or "the one who has understanding"
    * **the prudent**  - This describes a person who has good judgment or sense.
    * **folly**  - lack of good judgment; foolishness
    * **the folly of fools is deception**  - The foolishness of fools is that they think they are wise, when they are not.
