===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The way of the sluggard is like walking through a hedge of thorns**  - AT: "The way of the sluggard is like a hedge of thorns" The life of a lazy person has many painful obstacles like a path overgrown with thorns. (See [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **the path of the upright is a built-up highway** - The life of those who live to please God is smooth and does not have barriers or obstacles. (See [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
