===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The eyes of Yahweh**  - "Yahweh sees" (See [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **A healing tongue**  - "A person who can give healing with what they say"(See [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **A healing tongue**  - a person who speaks kindly and heals with what they say (See [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **a deceitful tongue crushes the spirit**  - Lying causes pain and hopelessness.
    * **a deceitful tongue**  - a person who lies (See [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
