===== Translation Notes: =====
"Wisdom" is continuing her speech.  Most verses in the rest of Chapter 9 are parallelisms. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

    * **abuse** the act of treating someone harshly
    * **reprove**  - gently correcting someone
