===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **foolish woman** The concept of foolishness is being portrayed as a woman just as "Wisdom" was portrayed as a woman in [[en/bible/notes/pro/09/01|9:01]] (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **she is untaught and she knows nothing**  - she does not know anything (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **untaught**  - not having learned
