===== Translation Notes: =====

The adulterous woman continues speaking to the foolish young man.
    * **perfumed my bed** - "put sweet-smelling scents on my bed" 
    * **aloes** - fragrant wood from an East Indian tree
    * **cinnamon** - a spice made from the bark of an Asian tree
