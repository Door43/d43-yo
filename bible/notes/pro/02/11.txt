===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Discretion will watch over you, understanding will guard you** - the way a soldier would protect a king. They do this to "rescue you from the way of evil" (2:12) and to "keep you from the immoral woman" ([[:yo:bible:notes:pro:02:16|2:16]]; See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]).
  * **Discretion** - being careful in actions and speech
  * **It will rescue you** - discretion and understanding will deliver the person who has them (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **perverse** - turned completely away from what is good
  * **abandon** - leave and never return to someone or something
