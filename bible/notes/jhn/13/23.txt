===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There was at the table reclining**  - In that culture people would eat at a low table while lying on their side. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|]])
  * **Jesus' bosom** - Jesus' chest
  * **Jesus' breast** - Jesus' chest
  * **the one whom Jesus loved**  - This refers to John.
