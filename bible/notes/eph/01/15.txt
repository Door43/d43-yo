===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Because of this** - “For this reason”
  * **your love for all those set apart for him** - Your love for all the believers in Christ. Alternate Translation: "your love for all the saints in Christ"
  * **I have not stopped thanking God** - This can be translated as a positive statement as "I continue to thank God". (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
