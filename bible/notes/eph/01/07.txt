===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** in his beloved one** - "in God's beloved one, Jesus Christ"
  * **we have redemption** -"We refers to all believers. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **riches of his grace ** - "greatness of God's grace" or "abundance of God's grace"
  * **in all wisdom and understanding** - God has given believers great wisdom and understanding. Alternate Translation "much wisdom and understanding."
