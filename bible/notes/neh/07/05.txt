===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **My God put into my heart**  - This is communication between God and man. It does not rely on a figure of speech to express it. Rather it relies on the author's belief that God is the source, and that the thoughts placed are consistent with his character.
    * **My God put into my heart** - Though God did not really put a thought into Nehemiah's physical heart, He caused Nehemiah to have this idea. AT: "My God made me think of an idea" (See: Metonymy) 
    * **to enroll**  - "to list, register, and organize"
    * **book of the genealogy**  - meaning an official book of parents and births that was part of Hebrew and many Semitic cultures.
    * **I found the book of the genealogy of those who returned at the first** - "I also found the records of the people who had been the first ones to return to Jerusalem" (UDB). Meaning an official book tracking the travels or migration of people within, or from outside the region or principality.
    * **and found the following written in it**  - This can be expressed as a new sentence. "The items that follow are what I found and selected from those records."
    * **bold words**  - explanation
