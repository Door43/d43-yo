===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Sanballat, Tobiah, and Geshem**  - These are all male names. (see: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **and that**  - "so that"
    * **no longer any sections broken open,**  - "no breeches", "no breaks"
    * **though up to that time**  - "except that", "although"
    * **Kephirim, Ono**  - geographic names. (see: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
