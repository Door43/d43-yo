===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the priests...repaired** - The implied information here is that the wall was being repaired. AT: "the priests fixed the wall" (See Implicit/Explicit)
    * **Benjamin and Hasshub repaired** - See above note for implied information. AT: "Benjamin and Hasshub fixed the wall"
    * **Benjamin**  - This is the name of a man. (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **Hasshub**  - This is the name of a man
    * **Azariah...repaired** - See above note for implied information. AT: "Azariah...fixed the wall"
    * **Azariah the son of Maaseiah the son of Ananiah**  - This is the name of a man
    * **son of Maaseiah**  - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **son of Ananiah**  - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18

    * **the son of Henadad** - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
