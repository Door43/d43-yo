===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Joiada...Paseah and Meshullam...Besodeia...Melatiah...Jadon** - These are all names of males. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **They set...the bolts** - Bolts are sliding bars used to lock a door or window.
    * **Melatiah the Gibeonite...Jadon the Meronothite..., men of Gibeon and Mizpah** These are the names of two males and the people groups to which they belong. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **the province beyond the River** - "the province near the Euphrates River" or "the near province at the Euphrates River"
