===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Malchijah...repaired** - The implied information here is that the wall was being repaired. AT: "Malchijah repaired the wall" (See: Implicit/Explicit)
    * **Malchijah**  - This is the name of a man (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **goldsmiths**  - "people who worked with gold and made gold things"
    * **temple servants** - These were people who worked in God's holy place of worship
    * **merchants**  - "sellers" or "traders"
    * **Appointment Gate**  - This is the name of an entranceway in the wall 
    * **upper living chambers**  - "the higher level rooms where people stayed"
    * **The goldsmiths and the merchants repaired** - See above note for implied information. AT: "The goldsmiths and the merchants repaired the wall"
    * **upper chamber**  - See previous note
    * **Sheep Gate** - This is the name of an entranceway in the wall
