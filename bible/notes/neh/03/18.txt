===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After him their countrymen repaired**  - The implied information here is that the wall was being repaired. AT: "On the next section of the wall their brothers did the repairs" (See: Implicit/Explicit)
  * **Bavvai the son of Henadad, the ruler of half the district of Keilah**  - Bavvai was the leader of half the district, not Henadad
  * **Bavvai the son of Henadad**  - this is the name of a man (See: How to Translate Names)
  * **the son of Henadad**  - This is part of Bavvai's full name (similar to a surname) but means "the male offspring of Henadad" or "whose father was Henadad"
  * **Henadad** - The meaning of the name is "Adad has been gracious"
  * **district**  - "governmental area" or "governmental region"
  * **Keilah**  - Keilah was the name of an area or region
  * **Ezer the son of Jeshua, the ruler of Mizpah**  - Ezer was the ruler of Mizpah, not Jeshua
  * **Ezer the son of Jeshua**  - this is the name of a man
  * **the son of Jeshua**  - This is part of Ezer's full name (similar to a surname) but means "the male offspring of Jeshua" or "whose father was Jeshua"
  * **Mizpah**  - this is the name of an area or region
  * **the ascent to the armory** - "the steps which went up to the armory" (UDB)
  * **armory**  - the place where weapons are kept
  * **buttress**  - "supporting corner" or "turning of the wall." The corner of a building where a supporting structure is.
