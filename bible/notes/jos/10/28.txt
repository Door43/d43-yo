    * **Makkedah** - called “a place of shepherds”.  A chief city of the Canaanites, near which five confederate kings were defeated, taken in the cave to which they had fled, and killed.  (See:[[en:obe:other:shepherd]], See:[[:yo:obe:other:canaan]], See:[[:yo:obe:other:king]]).
