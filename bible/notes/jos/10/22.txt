===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jarmuth**  - "Jarmuth" - A town in the low country of Judah.
    * **Lachish**  - "Lachish" - generally regarded as the second most important city in the southern kingdom of Judah. The city was surrounded by two walls including a lower retaining wall. 
    * **Eglon**  - "Eglon" - A town of Judah in the low country, known as the "city of palm trees".  (See:[[en:obe:other:palm]]).
