===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **the Jordan**  - A short name for the Jordan River. (See: [[:En:obe:other:jordanriver]])
    * **soles of their feet**  - Here the sole of a foot is used to refer the entire nation of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
    * **four days** - A number of days. (See [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
