===== Translation Notes: =====
    * **choose** - (See:[[en:obe:kt:appoint]])
    * **sole of your foot**  - Here the sole of a foot is used to refer the entire nation of Israel. AT: " Where ever your foot touches, that land will belong to Israel." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
    * **the Jordan**  - A short name for the Jordan River. (See: [[:yo:obe:other:jordanriver]])
    * **upstream** - moving in the opposite direction from which a stream or river flows
    * **heap** - a large, untidy or messy collection of items
