===== Translation Notes: =====
    * **got up** - arose
    * **early** - before an expected time
    * **morning** - the period of time between midnight and noon
    * **Shittim**  - a plain or valley in the land of Moab where the Israelites were encamped after their two victories over Sihon and Og, at the close of their desert wanderings, and from which Joshua sent forth two spies secretly to view the land and Jericho.
    * **camped** - remained or stayed in an area for a period of time
    * **the Jordan**  - A short name for the Jordan River. (See: [[:yo:obe:other:jordanriver]])
    * **cross over**  - "cross over" means to go to the opposite bank of the river. AT: "travel from this side to the opposite side of the Jordan." (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
