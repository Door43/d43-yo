===== Translation Notes: =====

Rehab talks to the two Israelite spies.

  * **melt away before you**  - A figure of speech describing the people's reaction to the Israelites, like ice melting into water. AT: "not resist you." (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
