===== Translation Notes: =====
 
    * **rope**- a piece of strong cord made by twisting material together
    * **window**- an opening in the wall or roof of a building to let in light
    * **Whoever goes out the doors of your house**  - Expresses a condition, creating a hypothetical situation. (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
    * **their own head**  - Figure of speech meaning the fault will be their own. (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
