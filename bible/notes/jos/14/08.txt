===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **heart of the people melt with fear** - Refers to the people of Israel and their reaction to the bad report of the spies in Numbers 13:31-33. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **completely followed** - past tense of "follow" which means to go or come after someone or something.
