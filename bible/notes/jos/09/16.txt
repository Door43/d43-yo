===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Chephirah** - one of the four cities of the Gibeonites, named afterwards among the towns of Benjamin. (Ezra 2:25;Nehemiah 7:29) (See:[[:yo:obe:other:gibeon]], See:[[:yo:obe:other:benjamin]])
    * **Beeroth**  - one of the four cities of the Hivites (See:[[:yo:obe:other:hivite]]).
    * **Kirjath Jearim** - Kirjath-Jearim. city of woods or forests, a Gibeonite town (See:[[en:obe:other:gibeon]])
