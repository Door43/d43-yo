===== Translation Notes: =====
 
  * **among you** - "you" refers to the whole nation of Isreal. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
  * **the priesthood**  - Refers to the positions of priests as a group. (See: [[en:obe:kt:priest]])
  * **half-tribe of Manasseh**  - Refers to the part of the tribe that received an inheritance before the crossing of the Jordan River.
  * **beyond Jordan**  - Refers to the area on the other side of the Jordan River. (See: [[en:obe:other:jordanriver]])
