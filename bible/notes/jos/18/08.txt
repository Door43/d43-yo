===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Go up and down**  - AT: Go north and south
    * **cast lots**  - A way of making decisions which removes the leaders choice often expecting God's control of the outcome.
    * **for you**  - A plural form referring to all those sent into the land. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
    * **seven sections**  - A number of areas of land. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
