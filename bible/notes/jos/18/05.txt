===== Translation Notes: =====

Joseph is continuing his speech to the children of Israel.

    * **divide**  - AT: separate
    * **house of Joseph**  - Refers to the entire family of Joseph, represented by his two sons, Ephraim & Manasseh because his father Jacob (Israel) assigned him a double portion inheritance through them. (See: [[en:obe:other:ephraim]] and [[en:obe:other:manasseh]])
    * **seven sections**  - referring to the number of land portions left that the men who were sent out wished to receive (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **cast lots**  - AT: "choose by chance" or "randomly assign." A random method of choosing to remove the choice from the leader's will, often done with the idea that God will decide the outcome.
