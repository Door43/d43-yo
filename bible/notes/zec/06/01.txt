===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I turned and lifted my eyes **- The prophet Zechariah continues describing his vision.
  * **four chariots**  - Chariots are two-wheeled vehicles (or "carts") usuallypulled by two or more horses.
  * **two mountains were made of bronze**  - This could be written as "two bronze mountains" or "two mountains of bronze." (See: Passive.)
