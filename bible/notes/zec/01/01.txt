===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Berechiah...Iddo**  - These are the names of males. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **second year of Darius' reign**  - "second year since Darius became king"
  * **exceedingly angry with your fathers**  - "very angry with your forefathers" 
  * **"Turn unto me **- The word "turn" is used to refer to a change.  Yahweh is telling the people of Israel to change from not obeying him to obeying him. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **"and I will return to you"**  - By saying he will return to the people of Israel, Yahweh is saying that they will have good things happen because he is helping them. AT: "and I will bless you" (See: [[:yo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
