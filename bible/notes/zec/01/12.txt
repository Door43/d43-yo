===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **how long will you show no compassion to **- AT: "How long will you withhold (or "hold back") your compassion (or "mercy") for"
  * **Jerusalem and to the cities of Judah that have suffered indignation**  - AT: "Jerusalem and all of Judah have felt your anger"
  * **Yahweh spoke kindly to the angel who had talked to me, saying things that comforted him.**  - AT: "Yahweh spoke words of kindness and comfort to the angel who had talked to me." \\
