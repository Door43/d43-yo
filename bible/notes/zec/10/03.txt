===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wrath burns against the shepherds; **- See (Metaphor) AT: "anger toward the leaders is strong"
  * **it is the male goats—the leaders—that I will punish;**  - AT: "and I, Yahweh, will punish them."
  * **Yahweh of hosts will also attend to his flock, the house of Judah,**  - AT: "I will be the leader of the house of Judah,"
  * **and make them like his war horse in battle! **- See (Simile) AT: "and will give them my mighty fearless strength."
