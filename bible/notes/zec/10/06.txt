===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will strengthen the house of Judah**  - See (Metonymy) "I will make the people of Judah strong" See (UDB)
  * **I had not cast them off**  - "I had not rejected them"
  * **Ephraim will be like a warrior,**  - See (Simile) "Ephraim will be very strong,"
  * **their heart will rejoice as with wine;**  - See (Simile)  AT: "and they will be truly very happy."
  * **their people will see and rejoice.  Their heart will rejoice in Yahweh! **-  AT: "Their children will see what has taken place and will also be happy because of what Yahweh has done for them."
