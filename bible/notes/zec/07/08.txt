  * **"Thus says Yahweh of Hosts; he had said,**  - '"Yahweh of Hosts had said,"
  * **widow**  -a woman whose husband has died.
  * **orphan**  - a child whose parents are dead.
  * **sojourner**  - a temporary resident (foreigner or traveler)
