===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But they refused to pay attention and set their shoulder stubbornly; **- See (Idiom) AT "But they intentionally refused to listen and stubbornly followed their own desires."
  * **stopped up their ears**  - See (Metaphor)
  * **They made their hearts hard as diamond**  - See (Simile) "They purposely determined to shut out (or ignore) Yahweh's messages to them." A diamond is a very hard clear stone.
  * **his Spirit**  - the Spirit of God or the Holy Spirit.
