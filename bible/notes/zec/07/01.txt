===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then in the fourth year of king**  - "During the fourth year of the reign of king"
  * **Darius**  - This is the name of the king. See (How to Translate Names)
  * **on the fourth day of Kislev**  - See (UDB).
  * **ninth month**  - "ninth month of the year"
  * **Sharezer **  and Regem-melech - See ( [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]]).
  * **they said,"Should**  - "they asked, "Should"
  * a fast - "not eating"
