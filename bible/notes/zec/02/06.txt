===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ho,**  - See (Hyperbole). This term is an exclamation of urgency used by prophets.  It is often translated as "get up", or "come".  The (UDB) uses "run" and "flee" to indicate the sense of urgency.
  * **land of the north**  - This refers to Babylon.
  * **scattered you**  - Could read "spread you abroad." or "exiled you."
  * **I have scattered you like the four winds of the skies**  - (See: Simile). AT: "I have exiled the people of Judah to many different places."
  * **Ho!  Escape to Zion,**  - AT: "Quickly leave Babylon and return to Zion (or "Jerusalem"),"** **
  * **you who live with the ****daughter of Babylon**  - "you people (or "Israelites") that are living in Babylon."
