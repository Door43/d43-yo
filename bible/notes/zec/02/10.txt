===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ho**  - See the explanation in v6.
  * **daughter of Zion**  - This refers to the people of Jerusalem and all of Judah.
  * **I myself am about to come and encamp among you!"**  - AT: "I am going to come and live with the people of Judah! (or "among you!")"  The word "encamp" means to set up and use a camp.
  * **nations will join themselves** to - "nations will make a union (or "pact") with"
  * **in that day**  - This refers to the day in which these events occur. Zechariah's prophecy may begin to be fulfilled in his own time, but ultimately will be fulfilled by the Messiah. "In that day" often refers to end times also.  Prophecy can also refer to and overlap multiple periods of time.
  * **in the midst**  - "in the middle."
  * **sent me to you.**  - "me" refers to the Messiah (Jesus) and/or to Zechariah.  "you" refers to Judah.
