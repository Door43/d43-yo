Translation Notes:

  * **will inherit Judah as his rightful possession**  - AT; "will choose Judah as his special possession".  "rightful" - means "to have a valid, legal, or just claim."
  * **his/himself/he**  - These pronouns refer to Yahweh.
  * **in the holy land**  - This term refers to Israel including Judah, which is in the southern part of Israel.
  * **choose Jerusalem for himself. **- "choose the city of Jerusalem for himself."  His temple is in Jerusalem.
  * **Be silent, all flesh, before Yahweh;**  - To "be silent" means "to make no sound."  AT: "Everyone be still and worship Yahweh;"
  * **for he has been roused from out of the heavens!**  - "roused" means to be "woken up" or "stirred" or "is paying attention."  AT: "for he is springing into action from his holy dwelling in heaven!"
