===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Joshua**  - See notes in Ch. 3:1.
  * **will walk in my ways,**  - or "will follow my ways'" or "will obey my ways"
  * **if you will keep my commandments,**  - or "carefully serve me," or "obey my laws"
  * **then you yourself will also adjudicate over my house and keep my courts;**  - or "then you will be given authority (or 'charge'; seeUDB) over my temple and it's courtyard;"
