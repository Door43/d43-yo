===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **showed me Joshua the high priest**  - This was not the son of Nun and Moses helper. Joshua was a common name and this Joshua lived much later in history.
  * **Is this not a brand pulled from the fire?"**  - See (Simile).  “This is a burning stick.”  A (brand) is a burning stick (brand) and could be used to threaten and hurt anyone when taken out of the fire.
