===== Translation Notes: =====

  *  **lifted my eyes**  - "looked up"
  * **I saw,behold, a flying scroll**  - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention. "and behold, I saw a flying roll of paper."  A scroll is a rolled up piece of paper or other material that can be written on.
  * **cubits**  - (See: [[:yo:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]]).
