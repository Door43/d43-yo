===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then he said to me**  - "he" refers to the angel who was talking with Zechariah.
  * **goes forth out over the face of the whole land, **- "is spoken (or goes out) to the whole nation."
  * **will be cut off**  - "completely removed (or 'banished')"
  * **based on what it says on the one side,**  - "it" refers to the scroll.  AT: "based on what is written on one side of the scroll paper,"
  *  **who swears a false oath**  - AT: "who lies"
  * **what it says on the other side**  - "what is written on the other side of the scroll paper
  * **according to their words**  - confirms that they spoke lies.
  * **'I will send it out,' Yahweh of Hosts declares,**  - AT: "Yahweh says, 'I will send the curse"
  * **'so it will enter into the house of the thief and into the house of the one that swears falsely by my name.**  - See (Metonym)  AT: "into the houses (or 'families') of the thieves and the liars."
  * **It will remain in his house and consume its timber and stones**  - The word "consume" is used here to show that nothing will be left behind after the curse is fulfilled. Everything will be completely destroyed. (See: [[:yo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]]). "The curse will remain in their family until nothing is left, not even the house!"'
