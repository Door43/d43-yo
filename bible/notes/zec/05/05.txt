===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **with me went forth and said to me -**  AT: "with me and said to me"
  * **see what is going forward**  - AT: "see what is coming next"
  * **a lead covering**  - This refers to a heavy cover. Lead is a heavy and soft metal.
  * **a woman under it sitting in it!**  - AT: "a woman sitting inside the barrel."
