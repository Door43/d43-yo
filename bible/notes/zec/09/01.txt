===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **An utterance of the word of Yahweh concerning**  - AT: "Yahweh's message about"
  * **Hadrach/Hamath**  - See (How to Translate Names)
  * **for Yahweh's is the eye on mankind,**  - AT: "for Yahweh is watching all mankind,"
  * **Also concerning Hamath,**  - AT: "Doom is certain for Hamath,"
  * **and Tyre and Sidon,**  - AT: "and for the people of Tyre and Sidon,"
  * **are very wise.**  - "are very shrewd."
