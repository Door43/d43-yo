===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **God will save them **- "God will rescue them"
  * **for they shall be the jewels of a crown,**  - AT: "and in our land they will be as beautiful as the jewels are in a crown**.**" See (Simile)
  * **how beautiful it will be!**  - "how beautiful they will be!"
  * **men will flourish on grain**  - "men will thrive (or "grow strong") on food they receive from the harvest"
  * **and the virgins on sweet wine!**  - AT: "and the young women will thrive on new wine!"
