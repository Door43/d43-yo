===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **built herself a stronghold**  - "built a strong fortress" or "built a high wall" See (UDB)
  * **heaped up silver like dust and refined gold like mud in the streets**. - See (Doublet) AT: "and valuable metals (or "gold and silver") are as plentiful as dust is in the streets. - See (Simile)
  * **Look! The Lord will dispossess her **- AT: "Pay attention! The Lord will take away Tyre's possessions,"
  * **and destroy her strength on the sea,**  - AT: "and destroy Tyre's ships that rule the sea (or "waters"),"
  * **so she will be devoured by fire.**  - AT: "and burn the city to the ground."
