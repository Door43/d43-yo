===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **'If something seemed impossible in the eyes of **  - "If something does not appear to be possible to"
  * **the remnant of this people**  - AT: "the people of Judah"
  * **should it also seem impossible in my eyes?**  - See (Rhetorical Question) AT: "it will surely be possible for me!"
  * **'Behold the one who rescues****my people****
 -**  AT: "I, Yahweh, will bring back my people"  The people of Judah who had been exiled.
  * **from the land of the sunrise and from the land of the setting sun**  - The people of Judah had been exiled earlier to the land of Egypt and Babylonia.
  * **so they will again be my people**  - The people would worship and live in a way that is pleasing to Yahweh.
  * **I will be their God in truth and in righteousness! **- AT: "I will be true and just toward them as their God!"
