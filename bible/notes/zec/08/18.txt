===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then the word of Yahweh of Hosts came to me and said,**  - AT: "Another message came to me from Yahweh of Hosts."  The word "me" refers to Zechariah.
  * **"Yahweh of Hosts says this: **- "He said,"
  * **'The fast of the fourth month,**  —-etc.—- **of the tenth month**  - AT: "The traditional fasts of the fourth, fifth, seventh, and tenth month of the year" or "The days you don't eat during the fourth…etc."
  * **will be joy and gladness and great goodness to the house of Judah!**  - AT: "will become festivals (or "celebrations") of joy to the people of Judah!"
  * **Therefore love truth and peace!'" **- This is a command to live as Yahweh has told them to live. or "You must love truth and peace."
