===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **'In those days ten men from every tongue and nation**- See (Hyperbole) and (Doublet) "'In those days many men from many (or "various") nations"
  * **will grasp the hem of your robe**  - See (Synecdoche) "will grab (or "grasp" or "clutch") your clothes to get your attention"
  * **and say, 'Let us go with you,**  - "and say, 'Please let us travel to Jerusalem with you,"
  * **for we have heard that God is with you!'"**  - AT: "for it is well known that God is blessing the Jewish people."'
