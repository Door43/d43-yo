===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **'Strengthen your hands,**  - See (Idiom) AT: "Be strong (or "resolute" or "brave") in your belief (or "attitude),"
  * **continue to hear now **- "continue to listen and remember"
  * **there was laid the ****foundation of the house**  - AT: "when the foundation of the temple was set in place"
  * **For before those days**  - AT: "In those days, before you started to rebuild the temple,"
  * **no crops gotten**  - AT: "there were no crops to harvest"
  * **were there crops for the labor of any beast.**  - "were there crops that needed animal labor to complete the harvest."
