===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On that day**  - This refers to the day that the armies attack Jerusalem.
  * **Yahweh's declaration**  - (See [[:yo:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:standard-notes:declarationofyahweh|declaration of Yahweh, declaration of the Lord]]).
  * **I will strike**  - To make something happen in a quick and dramatic way.
  * **horse with terror**  - "terror" means panic, strong fear, or fright.
  * **I will open my eyes in favor over the house of Judah**  - AT: "I will protect the people of Judah"
  * **say in their heart**  - This means they will think and say to themselves.
  * **because of Yahweh of hosts **- AT: "because they worship Yahweh of hosts"
