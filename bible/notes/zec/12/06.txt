===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In that day**  - Refers to the period of time when Jerusatem is attacked and God defeats the attackers.
  * **leaders of Judah **- AT: "clans of Judah"
  * **like fire pots ****among wood and like a****flaming torch**  - See (Simile) These two phrases describe things that can be used to start a roaring and destructive fire. In this context, they show how Yahweh will help the leaders of Judah destroy their enemies completely. (See: Doublet).
  * **will devour all the surrounding armies**  - AT: "will burn up the surrounding nations"
  * **Jerusalem will again live in her own place**  - "The people of Jerusalem will again live in their own City". (See: Personification).
