===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will be a spring - **"will be a living spring" (or "fountain")"
  * **for their sin **- AT: "to cleanse their sin"
  * **will be in that day **- refers to the time that the "living spring" is open.
  * **cut off the names of idols from the land**  - AT: "erase idol worship throughout the land"
  * **so they will no longer be remembered;**  - AT: "so that the names of false gods will be forgotten;"
  * **and also I will cause the false prophets and their unclean spirit to pass out of the land.**  - AT: "I will remove the false prophets and their impure spirits from the land."
