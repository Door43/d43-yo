===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Sword! Rouse yourself **- AT: "Awake, O Sword" This is a call to arms and violence.
  * **against my shepherd **- This may be the king, or government leaders.
  * **the flock will scatter **- The "flock" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **the lowly ones **- The exact definition isn't apparent. "lowly" ones could be the poor or disadvantaged, or even those who do not trust in God/Yahweh.
