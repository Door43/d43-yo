===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And how do you see it now?****
 - **  "See how different (or ulgy) and useless it looks now? See (Rhetorical Question).
  * **Is it not like nothing in your eyes**? - See (Simile). "It must look hopeless to you at this time."
  * **Now, be strong,**  - "From now on be strong,"
  * **my Spirit stood among you**  - "my Spirit was with (or 'within') you"
