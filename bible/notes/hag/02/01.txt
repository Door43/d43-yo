===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the seventh month on the twenty-first day of the month,**  - See (Biblical Time) in KT.
  * **came by the hand of Haggai - Haggai was the messenger.**  - See (Metonym) and (Synecdoche).
  * **Haggai, Zerubbabel, Shealtiel, and Jehozakak**  - See (How to Translate Names).
  * **remnant of the people. Say,**  - "remnant of the people. Say to them,"  These people had returned from captivity in Babylon.
