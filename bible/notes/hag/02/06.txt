===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **once again shake the heavens and the earth,**  - The word "shake" describes a very great disturbance, but does not tell us exactly what the disturbance will be like.
  * **every nation will bring their precious things to me, **- The nations appear to recognize that God is the author of the terrible disturbance and bring their valuable things to the temple as an offering appease (or satisfy) Yahweh.
