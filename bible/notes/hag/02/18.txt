===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month,**  - According to the Hebrew calendar.
  * **Consider it!**  - or "Think about it!"
  * **Is there still seed in the storehouse?**  - See (Rhetorical Question). "There is little or no seed in the storehouse."
  * **the fig tree, the pomegranate, and the olive tree**  - These are fruits grown in the land.
  * **have not brought forth! **- "have not produced fruit!"
