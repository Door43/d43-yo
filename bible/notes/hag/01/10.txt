===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the heavens - "the sky"**
  * **withhold the dew**  -"withhold the rains**"**
  * **a drought upon the land**  - The word "drought" means dryness that will hinder or prevent the growth of plants and dry up all the water that is needed for animals or people in that area.
  * **wine or oil**  - Refers to grapes and olives.
  * **labor of your hands!'" **- "all the hard work you have done!" See (UDB) and (Synecdoche) and (Metonymy).
