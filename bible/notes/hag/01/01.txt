===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **second year of Darius….sixth month…first day of the month -**  See (Biblical Time in KT).
  * **second year of Darius the king, -**  "second year of the reign of Darius the king,"or See UDB.
  * **came by the hand of Haggai -**  Haggai was the messenger. See (Metonym) and (Synecdoche).
  * **Darius….Haggai….Zerubbabel….Shealtiel….Jehozadak -**  These are all names of men. See (How to Translate Names).
