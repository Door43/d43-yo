===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **of all the remnant of the people**  - The word "remnant" refers to the people that were still alive after they arrived from their captivity in Babylon and started to rebuild Jerusalem.
  * **in the twenty-fourth day of the sixth month, in the second year of Darius the king.**  - See Translation Notes for Haggai 1:1&2.
